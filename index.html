ABOUT ME

  • Meet Manzooma Abidi, an experienced and versatile educator with a passion for coding and web development. With a Bachelor's degree in Technology from APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University, Manzooma has honed her skills in designing and developing dynamic and responsive websites, writing well-designed and efficient code, and using best software development practices. Currently, Manzooma works as a Coding Instructor at BYJU'S, where she equips students with the skills and knowledge to become successful coders in an increasingly digital and technology-driven world. Her responsibilities include creating engaging and interactive learning materials, communicating with parents, students, and faculty, and keeping up-to-date with emerging trends and best practices in coding education. In addition, Manzooma works as a Web Developer at Avadh Institute of Management and Science, where she designs, develops, and maintains visually appealing and user-friendly websites that meet the organization's needs and standards. She collaborates with a team of designers, content creators, and other developers to ensure that the website aligns with the organization's goals and reflects its brand image. Her skills include HTML and CSS, graphic design, interactive web design, user experience (UX) design, responsive design, typography, color theory, accessibility, SEO, customer experience, and business strategy. With her diverse skillset and passion for education, Manzooma Abidi is a valuable asset to any organization seeking a coding instructor or web developer.
